Havana Luz — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-25

Summary

Havana Luz does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data created by your use of the app is stored only on your device.

Data the app stores locally on your device

  • Your chosen dance styles and reminder preferences.
  • Your local attendance history.
  • Cached schedule data fetched from the dance school's public website.

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app deletes all of it.

Network usage

The app periodically fetches the public class schedule over HTTPS from the dance school's website. No personal information is sent in these requests.

Permissions

  • Notifications, exact alarms, full-screen intent: to deliver class reminders on time.
  • Boot completed, wake lock: to re-arm reminders after the device restarts.
  • Foreground service: for the short background sync that checks for instructor changes.
  • Internet: to fetch the public schedule.

Third parties

The app contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no crash-reporting services.

Children

The app is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated; significant changes will be reflected by the effective date above.

Contact

For questions or concerns, please open an issue at github.com/chenvaltzer/havanaluz-privacy/issues.
